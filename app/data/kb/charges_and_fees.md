# Common Banking Charges and Fees

- **ATM Withdrawals**:
  - First 5 transactions per month free at own-bank ATMs.
  - Charges apply for additional withdrawals.

- **Minimum Balance Penalty**:
  - Falling below minimum average balance may attract penalties.
  - Check your account type for applicable limits.

- **Cheque Bounce Charges**:
  - Applicable if cheque is dishonoured due to insufficient funds.
  - Varies by account type.

- **NEFT/IMPS/RTGS Charges**:
  - NEFT/IMPS: Free in many banks via NetBanking.
  - RTGS: May attract nominal fees depending on amount and channel.
