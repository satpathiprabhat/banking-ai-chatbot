# NetBanking – Common Troubleshooting Steps

- **Check Connectivity**: Ensure your internet connection is stable.
- **Browser Compatibility**: Use supported browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Edge). Update to the latest version.
- **Clear Cache & Cookies**: Clear browser cache and cookies to remove corrupted session data.
- **Disable Extensions**: Turn off ad-blockers or extensions that may interfere with login pages.
- **Try Another Device**: Attempt login from a different device to isolate device-specific issues.
- **Scheduled Maintenance**: Check for maintenance windows announced on the bank’s status page.
- **Account Status**: Ensure your account is active and not dormant. Dormant accounts may restrict online access.
