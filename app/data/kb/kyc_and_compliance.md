# KYC and Compliance

- **KYC Pending**:
  - Accounts with incomplete KYC may face restrictions on transactions.
  - Visit branch or use online KYC update portal to complete verification.

- **Document Upload Errors**:
  - Upload clear, valid documents in accepted formats (PDF/JPEG).
  - File size must not exceed system limits (typically 2 MB per file).

- **Dormant Accounts**:
  - Accounts may become dormant after 2 years of inactivity.
  - To reactivate, perform a transaction at branch or NetBanking after KYC verification.
