# FD Premature Closure – Policy Overview

- Premature closure may be **allowed** but can attract:
  - **Penalty** as per the bank policy.
  - **Lower applicable interest** (rate corresponding to the actual run period) rather than the booked rate.
- Partial closure may or may not be available; check product terms.
- Proceeds are typically credited to the **linked operative account** after closure.

## Digital Steps (if enabled)
1. NetBanking/Mobile → **Deposits** → **FD** → **Premature Closure**.
2. Select FD, review **penalty/interest adjustment**, and **confirm**.
3. Save closure advice for records.

If the option is not visible, visit the **branch** with valid ID proof.
