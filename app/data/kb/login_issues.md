# Login Issues – NetBanking

- **Forgot Password**:
  - Use the “Forgot Password” link on the NetBanking login page.
  - Reset password using registered mobile/email authentication.

- **Account Locked After Failed Attempts**:
  - Accounts may be locked after 3 consecutive failed password/OTP attempts.
  - Wait 30 minutes before retrying, or use “Forgot Password” to reset.
  - Contact customer support if still locked.

- **OTP Not Received**:
  - Verify your registered mobile number is active.
  - Check for SMS delays with your telecom provider.
  - Ensure “Do Not Disturb (DND)” is disabled for transactional SMS.
